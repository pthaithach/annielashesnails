About Annie Lashes & Nails

Promising Excellence in Everything We Do

Our mission at Annie Lashes & Nails is to build a lasting relationships with our clients through communications, honesty, and professional services. Our goal is to have our clients leave the nails salon feeling pampered and beautiful by performing superior customer services. We use disposable kits and liners for your safety. All instruments and tools are always disfected right after each client with the most effective methods. Please feel free to share your needs with us. If there is any questions about nails, eyelashes, facials, permanent make-up, wax or any complaints about your visit please contact (251) 767-7150.
